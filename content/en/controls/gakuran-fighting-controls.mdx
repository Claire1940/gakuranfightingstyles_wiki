<Section icon={Shield} title="Defensive Maneuvers and Survival">

Survival in Gakuran is dependent on your defensive literacy. If you only focus on the offensive side of **gakuran fighting controls**, you will struggle against experienced players who utilize parries and dodges. Defense is active, meaning you must anticipate the enemy's rhythm.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Guarding">
    Hold your block key when you see the opponent approaching. This reduces the chip damage from light attacks and prevents a full stagger.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Reading the Heavy">
    Watch for the "wind-up" animation. Heavy attacks usually have a distinct visual cue. This is your signal to either dodge or prepare a counter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Counter-Dash">
    Use your movement keys in conjunction with jumping or sprinting to "phase" out of the way of a heavy strike, then immediately follow up with your own M1 string.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Perfect Block Mechanic">
Timed blocks (blocking right as the hit lands) can often stagger the opponent, providing a massive window for a free combo. Practice this timing against NPCs before trying it in high-stakes PvP.
</Callout>

| Defensive Action | Control | Benefit | Risk |
