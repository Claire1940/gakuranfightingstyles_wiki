<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Inputs and Fighting Mechanics">

The heart of Gakuran lies in its PvP system. To excel in the various fighting styles available, you must memorize the combat-specific **gakuran pc controls**. The game utilizes a standard M1/M2 system common in Roblox combat games, but with specific timings that vary depending on your chosen fighting style. Mastering these inputs is the difference between being a school legend or a floor-mat.

| Combat Action | Input | Description | Tactical Advantage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Light Attack (M1)** | LMB | Fast, low-damage strikes | Best for starting long combos |
| **Heavy Attack (M2)** | RMB | Slower, high-impact hit | Breaks through weak guards |
| **Block** | F (Typical) | Reduces incoming damage | Essential for surviving rushes |
| **Dash/Dodge** | Q (Typical) | Quick directional burst | Provides brief invincibility |
| **Execute** | V / B | Finalizes a downed opponent | Prevents enemies from reviving |

When engaging in combat, do not simply mash the Left Mouse Button. Most fighting styles have a "finish" hit at the end of a 4 or 5-hit M1 string that sends the opponent flying. Learning the rhythm of your specific style allows you to pause your string or transition into a heavy attack to keep the pressure on.

<Callout type="warning" title="Guard Breaking">
Repeatedly blocking without moving will eventually result in a guard break. If your guard is broken, you will be stunned for several seconds, leaving you completely vulnerable to a full combo.
</Callout>

To master the flow of battle, follow this recommended engagement sequence:

1.  **Observation**: Use the mouse to track your opponent's movement. Do not rush in blindly.
2.  **Gap Closing**: Use **Shift** to sprint toward the enemy, then immediately let go to regain stamina.
3.  **Initiation**: Use a quick **LMB** strike to test their reaction.
4.  **Reaction**: If they block, prepare an **RMB** heavy hit to break their guard. If they dodge, use your own **Dash** to stay on them.
5.  **Execution**: Once the opponent's health is depleted and they are in a "downed" state, use the **Execute** key to finish the fight.
