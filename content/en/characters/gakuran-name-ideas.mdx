<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering Fighting Styles and Combat Mechanics">

Your fighting style is the core of your offensive capability. While there are legendary styles with a 1% drop rate, the "Epic" tier styles like **Boxing** and **Hakery** are currently dominating the competitive scene due to their utility and ease of use. Every style has specific perks, such as I-frames (invincibility frames) or guard piercing.

**Key Combat Concepts:**
- **Clashing/Grappling**: When two players attack simultaneously, they enter a grapple. The player who reacts first wins the shove, dealing neglected damage.
- **Block Chip**: Even if an opponent blocks, certain styles deal "chip" damage. High block chip percentages can whittle down a defensive player quickly.
- **Guard Pierce**: Attacks that ignore a portion of the opponent's block defense.

| Fighting Style | Rarity | Primary Advantage | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Boxing** | **Epic** | Uninterruptible M2s / I-Frames | Aggressive brawling |

| **Legendary** | **1% Drop** | Massive Damage / Unique Skills | Top-tier leaderboard play |
| **Basic** | **Common** | Balanced / No specific perks | Beginners learning clashing |
