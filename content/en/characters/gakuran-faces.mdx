<Section icon={Target} title="How to Reroll Your Character Expression">

To change your character's look, you must use the in-game reroll system. This process replaces your current face with a new, randomly selected one from the pool. Because the system does not allow you to save your previous look, you must prepare sufficient resources before attempting to roll for a specific style.

Farming the necessary resources is a key part of the process. Players can earn tokens by completing daily challenges, winning competitive matches, or participating in seasonal events. Having a large stack of tokens ensures you do not get stuck with an undesirable face when your resources run out.

Here is the step-by-step process to reroll your character's expression:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Customization Menu">
    Locate the customization area in the main game lobby. Interact with the customization NPC or open the corresponding menu tab to bring up the character editor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to the Faces Section">
    Select the head or face customization icon from the side menu. This action displays your current expression and shows the available reroll options.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check Your Reroll Resources">
    Verify that you have enough reroll tokens or in-game currency. Each attempt consumes a set amount of resources, so ensure you have a sufficient balance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm the Reroll Action">
    Click the reroll button to spin for a new expression. The system will instantly replace your current face with the newly rolled option.
  </Step>
</Steps>
