<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Progression and Tactics">

Progression in Gakuran is less about leveling up stats and more about building a reputation and mastering the movement system. Long-term players focus on "running the school," which involves controlling key areas like the basketball court or the main entrance. This requires a combination of combat prowess and the ability to form temporary alliances with other powerful players.

- **Crowd Control**: When facing multiple enemies, use the "Carry" mechanic to use a downed player as a shield or a distraction.
- **Stamina Management**: Never let your stamina bar drop below 20%. You need that reserve for an emergency dash to the Green Zone.
- **Visual Deception**: Wearing "newbie" clothing can sometimes lure aggressive players into a false sense of security, allowing you to execute a surprise counter-attack.
- **High-Ground Advantage**: While stairs are dangerous, holding the top of a stairwell gives you a significant advantage in physics-based knockbacks.
