To truly excel in the **gakuran pvp guide**, you must move beyond basic attacks and master the defensive triad: Blocking, Dodging, and Spacing. These mechanics allow you to negate damage and create "punish" windows where the opponent is vulnerable.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Mastering the Block">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Reactive Dodging">
    Use your movement keys in conjunction with dashes to sidestep heavy attacks. A successful dodge often puts you in the perfect position for a counter-attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Spacing and Footsies">
    Maintain a distance where you can hit the opponent, but they cannot hit you. This "sweet spot" varies depending on your fighting style's range.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Combo Execution">
    Link your M1 attacks into special moves or heavy finishers. Practice the timing in safe zones before attempting these in high-stakes school battles.
