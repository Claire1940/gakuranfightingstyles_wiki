<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Dynamics and Reach Optimization">

The "Melee Reach" factor is perhaps the most underrated aspect of the **gakuran height build**. In high-level duels, the player who can land the first hit (the "neutral" win) usually dictates the pace of the entire match.

A tall build allows for "Zoning." This is the act of keeping your opponent at the very tip of your weapon's range. Because your limbs are longer, your active hitboxes extend further out from your center point. This creates a "danger zone" that shorter players must navigate carefully.

However, the "Recovery" stat is the short build's secret weapon. In Gakuran, every attack has "end-lag"—the time you are frozen after a swing. Short builds have reduced end-lag, meaning if you miss an attack, you can block or dash much sooner than a tall player can.
