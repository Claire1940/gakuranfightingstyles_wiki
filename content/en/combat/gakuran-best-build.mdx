<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Combat Mechanics and Strategy">

Once you have chosen a style and learned the controls, the next step in perfecting your **Gakuran best build** is mastering the mechanics of engagement. Combat in Gakuran is a game of "read and react."

### Blocking and Dodging
Defense is often more important than offense. A well-timed block can stagger an opponent, leaving them wide open for a full combo. Conversely, dodging allows you to reposition and reset the fight if you are being overwhelmed.

- **Blocking**: Reduces incoming damage. Watch for the opponent's "Heavy Attack" (M2) animation, as these usually break guards.
- **Dodging**: Use movement keys and dashes to exit the opponent's range. This is your best tool against "Rushdown" styles.

### Combo Efficiency
The goal of every engagement is to maximize damage while minimizing the chance for the opponent to escape. 
1. **Initiate**: Start with a dash-in M1 or a long-range poke.
2. **Extend**: Follow up with a series of light attacks.
3. **Finish**: End the combo with a heavy blow or a style-specific finisher that knocks the opponent back.

| Strategy | Focus | Best Against |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rushdown** | Constant M1 pressure | Slower, heavy-hitting styles |
| **Bait & Punish** | Waiting for misses | Aggressive, predictable players |
| **Zone Control** | Maintaining distance | Short-range brawlers |
