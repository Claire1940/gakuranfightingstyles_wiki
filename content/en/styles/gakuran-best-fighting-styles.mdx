To utilize the **gakuran best fighting styles** effectively, you must master the control scheme. The game uses a standard Roblox layout but adds layers of complexity through "Shift-Lock" and "Blocking" interactions.

| **Sprinting** | **Left Shift** | Faster Movement | Use to bait out an enemy's heavy attack |
