<Section icon={Swords} title="Rare Rarity Styles Analysis">

The Rare tier is where most players begin their competitive journey. While these styles are easier to obtain than Epic or Legendary variants, the performance gap between them is substantial. In the current 2026 landscape, Muay Thai stands as the undisputed king of this tier, offering utility that rivals higher-rarity options.

Muay Thai focuses on aggressive pressure. Every hit deals posture damage, making it a nightmare for defensive players. Furthermore, the 55% chance to clash into a grapple during M1 exchanges provides a unique defensive-offensive hybrid mechanic that can turn the tide of a match. In contrast, Karate is often viewed as the most forgettable style due to its lackluster animations, despite its ability to ragdoll enemies with heavy attacks.

| Style | Heavy Attack Effect | Guard Utility | Animation Quality |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Epic Tier: The Competitive Meta">

The Epic tier contains what many consider the best overall styles in Gakuran. Boxing, in particular, has defined the competitive meta for months. Its defining feature is the inclusion of i-frames (invincibility frames) on heavy attacks, allowing players to punch through incoming damage without being interrupted.

Hakari offers a different but equally potent playstyle. It revolves around the "Momentum" perk. By landing a full M1 combo, your subsequent heavy attack deals triple damage. This makes Hakari the go-to choice for players who enjoy high-risk, high-reward gameplay and flashy, fluid animations.

| Style | Unique Mechanic | Best Build | Combo Potential |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Boxing** | I-frames on heavy | Universal | High |
| **Hakari** | 3x Damage Momentum | Short/Medium | Very High |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate M1 Combo">
    Start with a standard light attack string to build momentum or force a guard.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Openings">
    For Boxing, use the heavy attack to trade through enemy strikes. For Hakari, wait for the momentum buff to glow.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Heavy Finisher">
    Unleash the heavy attack. Boxing provides a two-punch combo that is difficult to parry, while Hakari delivers a massive single-hit ragdoll.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Boxing Mastery">

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Legendary Styles and New Kure Technique">

Legendary styles represent the pinnacle of rarity, offering specialized mechanics that can dominate specific scenarios. Wrestling is a powerhouse for crowd control, featuring hyper armor on grab attacks. This means once the grab animation begins, it cannot be interrupted by outside interference, making it lethal in team fights or crowded arenas.

The newest addition, the Kure style, introduces a brutal headbutt heavy attack. This move ragdolls enemies and applies a "parry lock" for two seconds, preventing the victim from defending against follow-up attacks. While currently hampered by some animation bugs in the 2026 version, its potential for low-health comebacks is unmatched due to the 15% total damage boost when below 25% health.

| Style | Crowd Control | Special Perk | Range |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Wrestling** | Excellent | Hyper armor on grabs | Medium |
| **Capoeira** | Good | Dash cooldown reduction | Long |
| **Kure** | Moderate | 2s Parry lock | Short |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Choosing the Right Style for Your Build">

Finalizing your build requires a deep understanding of how your character's physical attributes interact with style animations. Height is the most critical factor in Gakuran. Taller characters have longer limb reach, which synergizes perfectly with styles that have wide-swinging animations or grab mechanics.

Conversely, shorter builds have smaller hitboxes and often benefit from styles with fast recovery frames. The Basic fighting style, despite its Common rarity, remains a viable choice for short builds due to its superior posture regain and quick counterattacks.

| Build Type | Recommended Styles | Priority Stat |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Short** | Basic, Hakari, Boxing | Speed / Posture |
| **Medium** | Boxing, Hakari, Capoeira | Versatility |
| **Tall** | Wrestling, Muay Thai, Kure | Reach / Power |
