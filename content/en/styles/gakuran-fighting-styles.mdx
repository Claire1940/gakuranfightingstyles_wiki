<Section icon={Target} title="Rare Style Analysis and Comparison">

Moving into the Rare tier of **gakuran fighting styles**, players begin to see more specialized combat roles. This tier is where the distinction between aggressive and defensive playstyles becomes prominent. Selecting the right Rare style can often bridge the gap when facing opponents with higher-rarity kits.

Muay Thai is widely regarded as the pinnacle of this category. It is designed for aggressive players who prefer tall builds, offering a significant 30% bleed damage through guards. Its unique 55% chance to clash into a grapple when M1 attacks meet provides a tactical edge that other Rare styles lack. Conversely, the Slugger style is often viewed as a high-risk gamble, increasing damage dealt by 25% but also increasing damage taken by 10%.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Legendary Styles and Specialized Combat">

Legendary **gakuran fighting styles** are rare drops that provide specialized utility, particularly in crowd control (CC) and unique status effects. These styles often require specific build types to maximize their potential, such as the tall build requirement for Wrestling.

The newest addition, the Kure style, introduces a devastating headbutt heavy attack. While it lacks the hyper armor of Wrestling, it prevents the enemy from parrying for 2 seconds after impact. This creates a massive window for follow-up combos. However, players must be mindful of the Kure style's longer heavy attack cooldown, which requires more calculated timing than the spam-friendly Boxing style.
