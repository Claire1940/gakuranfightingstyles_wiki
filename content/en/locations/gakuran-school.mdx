<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Stamina Management">

Combat in this environment is fast-paced, brutal, and highly dependent on positioning. Unlike traditional fighting games with complex combo strings, survival here relies on basic melee strikes, hit-and-run tactics, and psychological warfare. Every punch you throw and every sprint you initiate consumes stamina. If you deplete your energy reserves entirely, you will be unable to escape or defend yourself, leaving you at the mercy of aggressive opponents.

To maximize your combat efficiency, you must learn to space your attacks. Landing consecutive hits will stun your opponent, eventually leading to a knockdown. Once an opponent is knocked down, they drop their equipped accessories, which can then be claimed by anyone nearby. However, overextending into a crowd often results in getting third-partied by other players looking for an easy cleanup.

### The Art of the Tactical Excuse

A fascinating social dynamic has emerged within the community: using the chat to claim a "misclick" after attacking someone. If you accidentally strike a powerful player or find yourself outmatched, quickly typing an apology can sometimes defuse the situation. While this tactic is not guaranteed to save you, it adds a layer of diplomatic strategy to the otherwise chaotic gameplay.

<Section icon={Target} title="Hazard Avoidance: Slippery Stairs and Safety Zones">

The physical environment of the school is just as dangerous as the players roaming the halls. The most notorious hazard on the campus is the slippery staircases. Due to unique physics configurations, running or jumping on the stairs often triggers an uncontrollable slipping animation, sending your character tumbling to the bottom. This slip mechanic inflicts massive damage and leaves you incapacitated on the floor, making you an easy target for nearby brawlers.

To navigate the school safely, you must master the transition between high-risk zones and designated safe areas. The Green Zone is a specially marked sanctuary where combat is disabled. Understanding how to reach this area during a pursuit is the most critical survival skill you can acquire.
